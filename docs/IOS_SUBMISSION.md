QUICK TEST PATH

2. After sign-in, tap \"New,\" then tap the drop zone to open the native iOS photo picker and select any short video (mp4/mov, up to 3 minutes).
3. Wait about 1-2 minutes while the video is analyzed.
4. A craft read appears: hook, pacing, framing, and payoff notes, plus one prioritized \"fix this first\" lever.

Note on output: this is an honest craft critic. If the model is not confident it can critique a given video accurately, it intentionally returns no read and tells the user so, rather than fabricating feedback. If a test clip returns no read, try a different short video with clear visuals and audio.

USER-GENERATED CONTENT & MODERATION (Guideline 1.2)
Uploaded videos are strictly PRIVATE to the uploading account. They are processed solely to generate that one user's own craft read and are then shown only back to that same user. There is NO social feed, NO user-to-user content, NO public gallery of uploads, NO comments, and NO sharing or publishing surface anywhere in the app. Because no user ever sees another user's upload, there is no avenue for objectionable user content to be distributed to others. Uploads are validated on receipt (file must decode as a short video, max 3 minutes, max 200 MB) and are rejected otherwise. The app includes an agreement to our terms (no objectionable or illegal content) and an in-app way to report a concern or request removal by emailing naadvar@gmail.com, to which we respond within 24 hours. The public \"Examples\" tab shows only our own curated sample reels, never user uploads.

PRIVACY, DATA USE & ACCOUNT DELETION (Guideline 5.1.1)
- Email is used only to sign you in and to save your reads to your account.
- The uploaded video and its transcribed audio are processed only to generate that user's own craft read (App Functionality). They are not used to personalize anything beyond producing that read, are never used to train third-party models, and are never sold.
- Captions and the generated read are stored to build that user's own private \"Creator DNA\" trend (Product Personalization).
- An optional one-tap \"was this helpful / not useful\" control on a read records that interaction to improve our analysis quality (Usage Data). It is never used for advertising or cross-app tracking.
- There is no advertising SDK, no analytics-for-ads, and no cross-app/website tracking. The App Privacy answers above reflect exactly this.

TEST ACCOUNT DELETION
Any account created during review (e.g. review@apple.com) and all of its uploads and reads can be deleted on request by emailing naadvar@gmail.com; deletion is permanent and completed within 24 hours.

WHY THIS IS A NATIVE APP (Guideline 4.2)
The app is built with Capacitor and BUNDLES the compiled web build on-device (it does not load a remote website). This gives instant launch, a persistent home-screen presence, and native integration with the iOS photo library for selecting a reel. Authentication uses a bearer token rather than a cookie specifically because the native webview runs at a different origin than the API.

PRODUCT SCOPE
Creative Director gives feedback on video craft only (hook, pacing, framing, on-screen text, payoff). It makes no health, medical, financial, legal, or virality/performance claims.
